Skills

Branding & Design

I specialise in branding and design, and believe that every user challenge can be met with ingenuity, allowing for solutions that not only address user needs but also elevate the brand experience. With a focus on graphic design, branding, UX research, UI design, and prototyping, I embark on each project with a dedication to balancing functionality and aesthetic appeal.

My toolkit includes proficiency in Figma and Adobe Creative Suite, providing me with the versatility to translate concepts into captivating visual narratives. Through a blend of creativity and strategic thinking, I strive to craft designs that not only resonate with users but also stand as a testament to the essence and identity of the brands I collaborate with.


User Empathy

One of my key strengths in UX design is empathy and my capacity to immerse myself in the user’s perspective. My favourite part of the empathy process is crafting the user persona, delving into the genuine individuals on the other side of the screen. Employing Miro for empathy maps enhances my comprehension of user desires and needs, providing a visual aid in the process. The “I like”, “I wish”, “what if” exercise serves as a valuable tool to delve into the user’s mindset, fostering ideation focussed on user-centric solutions.


Qualitative Interviews

In my UX design methodology, qualitative interviews play a pivotal role, and I approach them with a nuanced strategy. I not only seek to understand, but also prioritise putting interviewees at ease, creating an environment where they can freely share their insights. Crafting an interview script allows me to stay focused and extract valuable qualitative data, but I also like to encourage flexibility in the conversation to ensure I capture the little gems that can be found off script. During interviews, active listening is paramount, enabling me to uncover nuanced user perspectives and refine design solutions that authentically address their needs and aspirations.
